Privacy Policy

A formal privacy policy does not yet exist. But, we won't know anything about you except for basic account info, and what's on your current shopping lists.

Most stuff on your lists will probably be personal shorthand - "coffee", "cereal", "milk", etc, so it probably wouldn't have much value if we tried to sell it.

Plus, everything is open source, so you can poke through the source code to see if doing something nasty. The worst currently is using Google Analytics to get basic anonymous/aggregated usage statistics from folks without adblockers.
